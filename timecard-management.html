üìå Time Code Reference

Paid Time Off Codes

  • HOL Holiday: Pre-loaded, can float to preferred day
  • VCA Vacation: Planned time off
  • OUT Sick Leave: Employee illness (or family with FMLA)
  • FP Flex Personal: 1-2 days off (min 2 hours, not vacation extender)
  • EDU Education: Educational purposes (proof may be required)
  • BSTIP Bereavement: See eligibility rules

Special Codes & Rules

  • FMLA FMLA: Wipes out HOL - must re-add holiday
  • RTW: Ready to Work - only during business needs
  • Jury Duty: Must produce stamped summons
  • 3+ Days Absence: Doctor's note required
  • Float Holiday: Not forced to work holidays
  • Holiday Pay: Separate lines for Holiday 2.5x
