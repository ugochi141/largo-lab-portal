Phlebotomy Rotation Rules

Standard Rotation Cycle:
  1. Opener (6:00 AM): First staff member, responsible for temperature checks, station stocking, and tube expiration verification
  2. Processor: Full shift processing, must complete 10am run, manages pending list handoffs
  3. Runner: 30-minute specimen delivery schedule to laboratory
  4. Backup Processor: Flexible coverage role, assists during peak times
  5. Float: Covers breaks and provides additional support where needed

Break Schedule (Standard - Netta): 9:00 AM, 11:00 AM, 1:00 PM

Shift Handoff Protocol: Verbal communication required, log sheet completion, problem specimen tracking
