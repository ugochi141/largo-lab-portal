üìã SBAR Communication Technique

SBAR (Situation-Background-Assessment-Recommendation) is a structured communication technique that provides important information concisely for UBT teams and co-leads.

S - Situation

What is happening right now? Describe the current situation clearly and concisely.

B - Background

What led to this situation? Provide relevant context and history.

A - Assessment

What do you think the problem is? Share your professional assessment.

R - Recommendation

What action should be taken? Propose clear next steps or solutions.
