üìã How to Use Schedule Manager:

  1. Launch Schedule Manager - Roster Builder and confirm the status indicator shows Ready (green).
  2. Select the schedule date, choose a team (Phlebotomy or Laboratory), and pick staff from the roster or add a custom entry.

  5. Open the Daily, Visual Coverage, or Grid scheduler and navigate to the published date to confirm the updates.
